Phase 01
Audit
Get a complete picture of your finances

The first step is connecting Ned to your business bank accounts. Ned securely pulls your full transaction history and organizes every dollar — showing you exactly where money is coming from and where it's going, automatically and in real time. This financial baseline is the foundation everything else is built on.

What's included in your Audit
  • Secure, encrypted bank account connection via Ned
  • Automatic categorization of all revenue and expenses
  • Unified view across multiple accounts, cards, and payment methods
  • Up to 24 months of historical transaction import
  • Real-time sync so your data is always current
  • Custom category rules to match how your business operates
  • Duplicate detection and transaction cleaning
  • Instant summary dashboard on completion

Takes less than 10 minutes

Most owners complete their full account connection and first audit in under 10 minutes.

Bank-level security

Ned uses read-only access and 256-bit encryption. We can never move or modify your funds.

No manual entry

Transactions import and categorize automatically. Your job is to review, not to input.

Phase 02
Analyze
Understand where your business stands

Once your data is in, Ned helps you interpret it. You'll learn how to read your own financial patterns — which months are consistently strong, where costs have quietly crept up, and which parts of your business are generating (or burning) the most cash. Ned turns raw transactions into clear, actionable insight.

What Ned analyzes for you
  • Revenue trends across 3, 6, and 12-month windows
  • Expense breakdowns by category, vendor, and time period
  • Profit margin analysis by product or service line
  • Cash flow forecasting to surface shortfalls early
  • Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons
  • Automated anomaly and trend detection
  • Industry benchmarking vs. similar businesses
  • Customer and revenue concentration risk flags

Plain-language summaries

Every chart comes with a plain-English explanation so you always know what you're looking at.

Trend alerts

Ned proactively notifies you when a metric crosses a meaningful threshold — up or down.

Exportable reports

Share your analysis with an accountant, advisor, or lender with one click.

Phase 03
Execute
Get a playbook built for your situation

Analysis only matters if it leads to action. Based on the patterns Ned identifies in your specific data, you receive a curated set of resources, step-by-step recommendations, and next steps tailored to your business right now — not generic advice, but guidance that matches what your numbers are actually telling you.

What your playbook includes
  • Pattern-matched recommendations based on your actual data
  • Resources for cost reduction, revenue growth, and cash flow
  • Step-by-step action items ranked by estimated impact
  • Tools, templates, and calculators for your specific challenge
  • Connection to vetted advisors when you need expert help
  • Progress tracking to measure improvement over time
  • Playbook updates each month as your situation evolves
  • Scenario planning for growth, hiring, or downturns

Situation-specific

Recommendations are generated from your actual data patterns, not a one-size-fits-all checklist.

Prioritized by impact

Every action item is ranked so you focus energy where it makes the biggest difference first.

Continuously updated

As your financials change month to month, your playbook updates automatically to stay relevant.
