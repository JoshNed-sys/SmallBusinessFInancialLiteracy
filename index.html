Partnership Overview
Cash Compass by Ned

Your business owners need real financial clarity.
Now you have something to give them.

Cash Compass is a ready-to-deploy bundle of technology and resources that gives small business owners immediate visibility into their finances, a plain-language picture of what's working and what isn't, and a personalized action plan to move forward — in under two hours, with no financial background required.

What this is

Ned provides the analysis — connecting to bank accounts, organizing transactions, and surfacing financial patterns. Cash Compass provides the interpretation and the path forward: a web-based field guide that explains every metric in plain language, what it means for that specific business, and what to do about it.

Partner organizations bring it to the business owners they already serve. No financial expertise required on your end. The guide does that work.

"We built Ned because the tools for understanding your own business have always existed for companies with finance teams — and almost never for the owner running a restaurant or cleaning service on their own. Cash Compass is how we fix that at scale."

Phase 01
Audit

Business owners connect their accounts and get a complete financial picture — many for the first time.

Business owners link their accounts to Ned. Ned pulls up to 24 months of transaction history, categorizes every dollar, and produces a clean financial baseline — automatically, in under 10 minutes. No manual entry, no spreadsheets. Every business in your program now has an organized financial picture that didn't exist before.

What this covers

  • Secure bank account connection
  • Automatic revenue and expense categorization
  • Unified view across all accounts and payment methods
  • Up to 24 months of transaction history
  • Real-time sync as financials change
  • Custom category rules per business
  • Duplicate detection and data cleaning
  • Instant summary dashboard on completion

Completed in under 10 minutes

Low barrier for business owners. High completion rate for your program.

No manual entry

Data imports and categorizes automatically. Owners review — they don't input.

Read-only access

Ned can view transactions. It cannot move or modify funds.

For your organization: every business owner who completes the Audit arrives at their next advising session with a documented financial baseline. Your advisors walk in already knowing the business. That changes the whole conversation.

Phase 02
Analyze

Ned interprets the data. Business owners understand their numbers — in plain language, without an advisor in the room.

Cash Compass provides the interpretation layer on top of Ned's analysis. For every metric and data point the analysis surfaces, the field guide tells the owner what it is, what it means for their business specifically, what healthy looks like versus what's a warning sign, and what questions to ask themselves. This is not generic financial education. It's contextual, diagnostic guidance tied directly to what their own data showed.

What Ned surfaces for each business owner

  • Revenue trends over 3, 6, and 12-month windows
  • Expense breakdowns by category, vendor, and period
  • Profit margin by product or service line
  • Cash flow forecasting before shortfalls become crises
  • Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons
  • Automated anomaly and trend detection
  • Industry benchmarking against similar businesses
  • Revenue concentration and customer risk flags

Plain-language explanations

Every metric comes with a plain-English explanation. No financial background required.

Diagnostic, not generic

Guidance is tied to what the owner's own data actually showed — not a standard curriculum.

Exportable for advising

Business owners can share their full analysis with your advisors in one click.

For your organization: business owners show up to advising sessions already knowing their numbers. Your advisors skip the diagnostic work entirely and focus on the highest-value conversation instead.

Phase 03
Execute

Every business owner walks away with a specific, personalized action plan — built from their own numbers.

Based on the patterns in each business owner's data, Cash Compass generates a tailored set of resources and prioritized next steps — specific enough to act on this week, tied to what the analysis actually flagged. This is the deliverable. Every participant who completes Cash Compass walks away with something documented and actionable that your organization can track, report, and build on in future sessions.

What each action plan includes

  • Recommendations drawn from their actual data patterns
  • Resources for cost reduction, revenue growth, and cash flow
  • Action items ranked by estimated impact
  • Tools and templates matched to their specific challenge
  • Progress tracking as financials evolve
  • Monthly updates as the business changes
  • Scenario planning for growth, hiring, or downturns
  • Pathways for deeper advising when needed

Common questions

FAQ

How does Cash Compass fit into our existing programs? ⌄
Cash Compass is designed to slot into whatever format you already use — cohorts, one-on-one advising, workshops, or self-paced access. We work with each partner organization to figure out the integration that makes sense for your community and program structure. There's no requirement to rebuild what you're already doing.
How does this work as a philanthropic program? ⌄
A philanthropy or community foundation funds access to Cash Compass for their grantee network — delivered at no cost to the partner organizations or business owners in that network. Cash Compass runs as the program infrastructure. Outcomes data flows back to the funder for reporting. We work with each funder to define what success looks like in their specific portfolio and how they want to measure it.
What does reporting look like at the portfolio or cohort level? ⌄
Partner organizations receive cohort-level completion data, financial baseline summaries, and action plan outputs across their business owner population. This data is designed for funder reporting, program evaluation, and advising prioritization — so you can see which business owners need the most attention and show your funders what actually changed.
What does partnering with Cash Compass involve? ⌄
Cash Compass is offered to business owners through partner organizations at no direct cost to the business owner. For partner organizations and philanthropic funders, we work through a deployment model based on your network size and program structure. Reach out and we'll walk you through what the partnership looks like in practice — no commitment required.
How much time does the program take for business owners? ⌄
Most business owners complete all three phases in under two hours total. The Audit takes under 10 minutes. The Analyze phase — reviewing the Cash Compass field guide for their specific metrics — typically takes 30–45 minutes. The Execute phase is ongoing: action plans update monthly as the business changes, making it a continuous resource rather than a one-time exercise.
Do business owners need any financial background? ⌄
No — and that's the whole point. Cash Compass is written specifically for business owners who are not financial professionals. Every metric is explained in plain language. Every recommendation is specific enough to act on without an advisor translating it. Your advisors are there for support and deeper work — not to interpret the basics.
Is business owner financial data secure? ⌄
Yes. Ned uses read-only bank connections — we can view transaction history but cannot move, modify, or access funds. Business owner data is never sold or shared with third parties, advertisers, or your organization without the owner's explicit consent.
