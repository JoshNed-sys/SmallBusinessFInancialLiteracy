Common questions

FAQ

How does Ned fit into our existing programs? ⌄
Ned is designed to be embedded into whatever format you already use — cohorts, one-on-one advising, workshops, or self-paced access. We work with each partner organization to figure out the integration that makes sense for your business owner base and program structure. There's no requirement to restructure what you're already doing.
What does partnering with Ned involve? ⌄
Ned is offered to small business owners through partner organizations at no direct cost to the business owner. We work with each organization to figure out what makes sense for their programs and community. Reach out and we'll walk you through what the partnership looks like in practice.
How much time does the program require from business owners? ⌄
Most business owners complete all three phases in under two hours total. The Audit takes under 10 minutes. The Analyze phase typically takes 30–45 minutes. The Execute phase is ongoing — playbooks update as financials evolve, making it a continuous resource rather than a one-time exercise.
Do business owners need any financial or technical background? ⌄
No — and this is by design. Ned is built specifically for business owners who are not financial professionals. Every metric, chart, and recommendation uses plain language with supporting explanations. If something is unclear, help is always available, and your advisors are there to support business owners through the process.
Is business owner financial data secure? ⌄
Yes. Ned uses read-only bank connections — meaning we can view transaction history but cannot move, modify, or access funds. Client data is never sold or shared with third parties, advertisers, or your organization without the business owner's explicit consent.
Can the program be co-branded with our organization? ⌄
Yes. Ned offers white-label options so the business owner-facing experience can carry your organization's identity. Your business owners stay in your ecosystem — Ned powers what happens underneath. Details vary by partnership level.
