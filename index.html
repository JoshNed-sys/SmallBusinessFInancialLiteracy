Audit

Business owners connect their accounts and get a complete financial picture — many for the first time.

Business owners link their accounts to Ned. Ned pulls up to 24 months of transaction history, categorizes every dollar, and produces a clean financial baseline automatically — in under 10 minutes. Every business in your program now has an organized financial picture that didn't exist before.

  • Secure bank account connection
  • Automatic revenue and expense categorization
  • Unified view across all accounts and payment methods
  • Up to 24 months of transaction history

  • Instant summary dashboard on completion

Common questions

FAQ

How does Cash Compass fit into our existing programs? ⌄
It slots into whatever format you already use — cohorts, one-on-one advising, or self-paced access. We work with each partner to figure out the integration that makes sense. No requirement to rebuild what you're already doing.
How does this work as a philanthropic program? ⌄
A philanthropy or foundation funds access for their grantee network — delivered at no cost to partner organizations or business owners. Cash Compass runs as the program infrastructure. Outcomes data flows back to the funder. We work with each funder to define what success looks like in their portfolio.
What does reporting look like at the cohort level? ⌄
Partner organizations receive cohort-level completion data, financial baseline summaries, and action plan outputs across their business owner population — designed for funder reporting, program evaluation, and advising prioritization.
How much time does the program take for business owners? ⌄
Most complete all three phases in under two hours. The Audit takes under 10 minutes. Analyze typically takes 30–45 minutes. Execute is ongoing — action plans update monthly as the business changes.
Do business owners need any financial background? ⌄
No. Cash Compass is written specifically for owners who are not financial professionals. Every metric is explained in plain language, specific enough to act on without an advisor translating it.
Is business owner financial data secure? ⌄
Yes. Ned uses read-only bank connections — we can view transaction history but cannot move or modify funds. Business owner data is never sold or shared without the owner's explicit consent.
