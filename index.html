Phase 01
Audit
Your clients get a complete picture of their finances — many for the first time.

In Phase 1, your clients connect their business bank accounts to Ned. From there, Ned automatically pulls their full transaction history and organizes every dollar — no manual entry, no spreadsheets. Within minutes they have a clear baseline of where their money comes from and where it goes. This is the foundation every subsequent conversation with your team can be built on.

What's included in the Audit phase
  • Secure, encrypted bank account connection via Ned
  • Automatic categorization of all revenue and expenses
  • Unified view across multiple accounts, cards, and payment methods
  • Up to 24 months of historical transaction import
  • Real-time sync so your data is always current
  • Custom category rules to match how your business operates
  • Duplicate detection and transaction cleaning
  • Instant summary dashboard on completion

Phase 02
Analyze
Your clients understand their numbers — and what those numbers mean.

Once their data is in, Ned does the interpretation work. Clients see which months are strong, where costs have quietly crept up, and which parts of their business are profitable — all in plain language. For your advisors, this means clients show up to conversations already knowing their situation. No more starting from scratch every session.

What Ned analyzes for each client

Common questions

FAQ

Is my bank supported? ⌄
Ned connects to thousands of U.S. financial institutions including major national banks, regional banks, credit unions, and online banks. If you're unsure, you'll be able to search for your institution during setup — we support the vast majority of business banking accounts.
Is my financial data safe? ⌄
Yes. Ned uses read-only bank connections — we can view your transaction history but cannot move, modify, or access funds. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties, advertisers, or your partner organization without your explicit consent.
How much does Ned cost? ⌄
Ned is offered to business owners through partner organizations — in most cases at no cost to you, subsidized by the partner. If you've been referred to Ned by a CDFI, SBDC, chamber, or other organization, access is likely included as part of their programming. Contact your referring organization for details.
How long does the program take? ⌄
Most business owners complete all three phases in under two hours total. The Audit takes less than 10 minutes, the Analyze phase typically takes 30–45 minutes to review and understand your data, and the Execute phase is ongoing — your playbook updates monthly as your financials evolve.
Do I need accounting knowledge? ⌄
No. Ned is specifically designed for business owners who are not accountants. Every chart, metric, and recommendation is written in plain English. If something is unclear, there's always an explanation available — and your partner organization's advisors are there to support you as well.
I'm a partner organization — how do I bring Ned to my clients? ⌄
