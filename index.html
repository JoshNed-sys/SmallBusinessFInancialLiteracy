Cash Compass by Ned

Your business owners need financial clarity.
Now you have something to give them.

A ready-to-deploy program that gives small business owners immediate financial visibility, a plain-language picture of what's working and what isn't, and a personalized action plan — in under two hours, no financial background required.

What this is

Ned provides the analysis — connecting bank accounts, organizing transactions, surfacing financial patterns.

Cash Compass provides the interpretation: a field guide explaining every metric in plain language and what to do about it. Partner organizations bring it to the businesses they serve.

"We built Ned because the tools for understanding your own business have always existed for companies with finance teams — and almost never for the owner running a restaurant or cleaning service on their own. Cash Compass is how we fix that at scale."

Phase 01
Audit

Business owners connect their accounts and get a complete financial picture — many for the first time.

Ned pulls up to 24 months of transaction history, categorizes every dollar, and produces a clean financial baseline — automatically, in under 10 minutes. Every business in your program now has an organized financial picture that didn't exist before.

  • Secure bank account connection
  • Automatic revenue and expense categorization
  • Unified view across all accounts and payment methods
  • Up to 24 months of transaction history
  • Real-time sync as financials change
  • Instant summary dashboard on completion

For your organization: every owner who completes the Audit arrives at their next advising session with a documented financial baseline. Your advisors walk in already knowing the business.

Phase 02
Analyze

Ned interprets the data. Owners understand their numbers in plain language — without an advisor in the room.

For every metric, the Cash Compass field guide explains what it is, what healthy looks like versus a warning sign, and what questions the owner should ask themselves. Contextual and diagnostic — not generic financial education.

  • Revenue trends over 3, 6, and 12-month windows
  • Expense breakdowns by category and vendor
  • Profit margin by product or service line
  • Cash flow forecasting before shortfalls become crises
  • Automated anomaly and trend detection
  • Industry benchmarking against similar businesses

For your organization: owners show up to advising sessions already knowing their numbers. Your advisors skip the diagnostic work and go straight to the highest-value conversation.

Phase 03
Execute

Every owner walks away with a specific, personalized action plan — built from their own numbers.

Based on each owner's data, Cash Compass generates prioritized next steps specific to their situation — concrete enough to act on this week. Every participant leaves with something documented and actionable your organization can track and report.

  • Recommendations drawn from their actual data patterns
  • Action items ranked by estimated impact
  • Resources matched to their specific challenge
  • Monthly updates as the business changes

For your organization: every completed session produces a documented action plan — the outcome you report to funders, not hours of TA.

Common questions

FAQ

How does Cash Compass fit into our existing programs? ⌄
It slots into cohorts, 1:1 advising, or self-paced access. We work with each partner to figure out what makes sense. No requirement to rebuild your existing programming.
How does this work as a philanthropic program? ⌄
A philanthropy funds access for their grantee network at no cost to partner organizations or business owners. Cash Compass runs as the program infrastructure. Outcomes flow back to the funder. We work with each funder to define what success looks like in their portfolio.
What does reporting look like at the cohort level? ⌄
Partner organizations receive completion data, financial baseline summaries, and action plan outputs across their population — designed for funder reporting, program evaluation, and advising prioritization.
How much time does it take for business owners? ⌄
Under two hours total. The Audit takes under 10 minutes. Analyze takes 30–45 minutes. Execute is ongoing — plans update monthly as the business changes.
Is business owner financial data secure? ⌄
Yes. Ned uses read-only connections — we can view transaction history but cannot move or modify funds. Data is never sold or shared without the owner's explicit consent.
