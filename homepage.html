Built for the way small businesses actually work

Most financial tools show you numbers without telling you what to do with them. Ned is different. We connect directly to your bank accounts, surface what matters, and give you a clear playbook to act on — all in one program.

Phase 01
Audit

Get a complete picture of your finances

The first step is connecting Ned to your business bank accounts. Ned securely pulls your transaction history and organizes it so you can see exactly where your money is coming from and where it's going — automatically, in real time.

  • Secure bank account connection via Ned
  • Automatic transaction categorization across revenue and expenses
  • Unified view across multiple accounts and payment methods
  • Historical data import so you're not starting from zero
Phase 02
Analyze

Understand where your business stands

Once your data is in, Ned helps you interpret it. You'll learn how to read your own financial patterns — which months are strong, where costs are creeping up, and which parts of your business are the most (and least) profitable.

  • Revenue trends visualized over 3, 6, and 12-month windows
  • Expense breakdowns by category and vendor
  • Cash flow forecasting to anticipate shortfalls before they hit
  • Margin and profitability insights by product or service line
  • Benchmarks to compare your performance against similar businesses
Phase 03
Execute

Get a playbook tailored to your situation

Analysis is only useful if it leads to action. Based on the patterns Ned identifies in your data, you'll receive a curated set of resources, recommendations, and next steps built specifically for your business's situation right now.

  • Pattern-based recommendations (e.g. revenue declining over 12 months → specific action plan)
  • Guided resources for cost reduction, revenue growth, and cash flow management
  • Step-by-step action items prioritized by impact
  • Access to tools, templates, and expert connections relevant to your challenge
  • Progress tracking to measure improvement over time
